History

ABS enterprises, inaugurated in year 2000, established with the objective to provide Manpower, Housekeeping & Security services to the Corporate & Public sector. Since the founder of the company, Mr. Santosh Kumar, is a retired officer from a chain of International Five star hotel, possessing 35 years of rich experience in hospitality Industry. His rich experience majorly in Housekeeping, Vigilance & in Front office given him immense confidence & motivation to set a start up which should serve public & private organizations. These were little & big achievements of a single person along with team, which has made ABS enterprises a formidable competitor in Delhi NCR.


ABS, later came up with a concept of Infrastructure Development, providing end to end solution of Construction, Civil & Plumbing, Heavy Electrical, Office renovation and Modular furniture.


Today, ABS is a Human Capital & Facility Management Organization, associated to provide end to end solution for business requirement such as Infrastructure Development, Facility management, Manpower support, House Keeping Services and Corporate security. ABS specialized in providing permanent & Contractual workforce, Blue collar workers, Production Operators/Helpers, Housekeeping and Pantry staff, Corporate Security, Horticultural staff and Drivers. We also provide qualified and experienced professionals for Promotional Events, Conferences, Exhibitions and various other theme based events.


Since 2000, ABS has been consistently instrumental in offering a comprehensive range of services to the different industries. Over the last decade, ABS enterprise has served different organizations in different industries with excellence & gain an award of trust.

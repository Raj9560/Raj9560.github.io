About the Company

ABS Enterprises is a Human Capital organization and Placement Agency organized to provide manpower consultancy services. Since 2000, we have been consistently instrumental in offering a comprehensive range of services. We are appearance of Human assets who serves for Trust, ethics with high determination, who also believes to provide value to our client’s business with quality.


We are specializes in providing permanent & temporary staff in areas of Marketing, Sales, Events, conferences, accounts, HR, administration, cleaning, facility management , housekeeping & security. These services are managed by highly qualified & professional white & blue collar manpower. We cater to special personnel needs of clients in the various industries.


ABS is sure enough that with the impeccable and time proven expertise of providing manpower having distinguished roles to various industries be able to put up an exemplary and exclusive style of working in sync with our integrity and commitment values.
